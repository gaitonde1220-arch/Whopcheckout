The problem

Standard Whop embeds don't scale

Whop's checkout is powerful, but embedding it on WooCommerce has always meant creating a separate plan-id for every product, price point, and variant you sell.

For stores with multiple products or configurations, this quickly becomes unscalable and frustrating.

The solution

A smarter way to checkout

WhopCheckout connects Whop directly to WooCommerce. The plugin auto-generates a checkout session for whatever's in the cart.

Stop fighting with manual Whop plan IDs. Let the plugin handle checkout dynamically.

  • No manual plan-ids
  • No configuration bottlenecks
  • Just seamless payments

Process

How it works

Five steps from cart to confirmed order.

1

Customer adds products to cart

Your customer browses your WooCommerce store and adds any products to their cart.

2

Select Whop at checkout

At checkout, they select Whop Checkout as their payment method.

3

Plugin generates checkout session

WhopCheckout automatically creates a Whop checkout session with the exact order total.

4

Customer completes payment

The customer pays through Whop's secure hosted checkout — cards, crypto, and more.

5

Order confirmed automatically

A signed webhook marks the order paid in WooCommerce. You're ready to fulfill.
